About Eminem

Eminem is one of the best-selling artists in music history, and easily one of rap's biggest crossover successes. He was the first white rapper since to garner both sales and critical respect, but his impact has exceeded this confining distinction. On sheer technical skills, Eminem is one of the greatest MCs of his generation: rapid, fluid, dexterous, and unpredictable, capable of pulling off long-form narratives or withering asides.
